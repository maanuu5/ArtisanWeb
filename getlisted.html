Join Our Global Artisan Community

Share your craft with the world and earn fair compensation for your traditional artistry. Follow these simple steps to get started on ArtisanMarket.

1

Create Your Artisan Profile

Tell your story and share your crafting journey. Complete your profile with information about your traditions, techniques, and the cultural significance of your craft. Upload clear photos of yourself at work and your workshop to build trust with customers.

2

Verification Process

Our team reviews your application to ensure authenticity and quality standards. We may schedule a video call to learn more about your craft techniques and verify your workshop. This step typically takes 3-5 business days as we carefully review each application.

3

Product Photography Guidelines

Learn how to showcase your products effectively with our photography guidelines. We'll help you capture high-quality images that highlight the craftsmanship and details of your work. You can also request our photography service in select regions for professional product shots.

4

List Your Products

Upload detailed information about each product, including materials, dimensions, creation process, and cultural significance. Set your own prices, keeping in mind our fair trade principles. Our platform suggests pricing based on labor, materials, and market research to ensure fair compensation.

5

Manage Orders & Shipping

Receive notifications when customers place orders. Package items carefully following our guidelines for safe international shipping. Print shipping labels directly from our platform and track deliveries. We handle customs documentation to make shipping simple for you.

6

Receive Payments

Get paid securely through our platform. Choose from multiple payment methods including bank transfers, mobile payments, or community payment centers in remote areas. Payments are processed within 3 business days of order delivery, with no hidden fees or complicated processes.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of artisans can join ArtisanMarket?

We welcome traditional craftspeople working in textiles, ceramics, woodwork, metalwork, jewelry making, and other traditional crafts. Our focus is on handmade items that reflect cultural heritage and traditional techniques.

Are there any fees to join the platform?

There is no fee to apply or maintain your profile. We only charge a 15% commission on completed sales, which covers payment processing, marketing, and platform maintenance.

How long does the verification process take?

Typically 3-5 business days. For artisans in remote areas, we work with local community centers to facilitate the verification process and can accommodate different communication methods.

What kind of support do you provide for shipping internationally?

We provide shipping labels, customs documentation assistance, packaging guidelines, and discounted shipping rates through our partnerships with global carriers. We also offer training on international shipping best practices.
